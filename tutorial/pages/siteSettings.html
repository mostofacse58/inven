Site Settings

Site Title: It will be shown in title of the site in browser

Footer: To show in site footer

Base Color:This color will change the color of your site in front end and backend

Phone:To show in front end

Email:To show in front end

Address: To show in front end

Date Format: The format of date you use

Currency: Your currency symbol

Country Time Zone: Your time zone

Available Language for Frontend: You may have 4 Languages but you may make only 2 languages available for front end customers

Facebook Link: Link of your facebook page

Twitter Link: Link of your twitter page

Pinterest Link: Link of your pinterest account

Slideshows

You can manages slideshows of your front page here

Delivery Time Range

You can set in which time of which day you can do your delivery

Delivery Charge Setup

You can set delivery charges based on your customer’s order amount

SMTP Email Setting

You can configure your SMTP email and set in which even who will get the notification

SMS Setting

You can configure your SMS email and set in which even who will get the notification

Pages

You can manage all of your front end pages from here

You can also manage your menu order by clicking on Order Page button

Areas

You can manage all of your deliverable areas from here. The purpose of area is to assign appropriate delivery person.

Payment Setting

Here you can configure and/or enable or disable payment methods you want for your customers.

Site LogoTo set your site logo.

Login Page BackgroundTo set your admin panel’s login page background.

Favicon:To set your site favicon.
