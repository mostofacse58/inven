Product

Units: You can manage your product units from here. Please go to Product->Units from left menu.

Product Categories: You can add all of your product categories from here. Please go to Product->Product Categories from left menu.

You can choose an icon(1) and color of an icon(2) that will be shown in the left menu in front end and you can also order categories to show in which order they will be placed in left menu(3). And if you select a category as Special Category that will be shown above the category in different colored area(4).

Product Subcategories: You can add all of your product subcategories from here. Please go to Product->Product Subcategories from left menu. You can choose your desired icon here too.

Products: You can add all of your products from here. Please go to Product->Products from the left menu.

Let us clarify some few things in Add Product: Though everything is self explaining but we think it would be better if you take a look on some newly added fields.

Name in Your Language: Just write the product name in your language so that your customers can search the product by typing his own language.

Name in People Language: Like Potato is আলু in Bengali but some people write it alu, so you can set that name too who does not know english enough and also can not type in his own language can search product in this way.

Purchase Unit, Sale Unit and Conversion Rate: In grocery business, the reality is maximum items are purchased in an unit but be sold in different unit. Like you purhcase potato by big bag but sale in KG. And that big bag may contain 50KG potato. In this case you will select Big Bag as Purchase Unit and KG as Sale Unit and the Conversion Rate will be 50. And you should set Purchase Price of 50KG in Purchase Price But if you purchase and sale something in same unit then you can select same unit in both dropown and enter 1 in Conversion Rate.

Description: Write a small description here so that people can understand about it quickly. Like: 50g each.

Discount Price: When a discount will be run you can put discount price here then the main price will be in strikethrough and discount price will be shown.

Has Offer: If you select Yes then it will be shown at the top of all products in offer products area.

Manage Stock: You will not be able to stock some products sometime, your delivery boy may purchase them when goes to delivery. So you will select No in this field so that the customem can order that product even that is not available in the store.

Product Upload: You can upload product by filling up an excel file using this. But it is recommended to add some test product using the form before using the excel upload.
